Sol Retreat

Nestled among ancient pine forests at 7,000 feet, Sol Retreat is our mountain sanctuary offering breathtaking views and rejuvenating solitude. This eco-luxury haven combines sustainable architecture with luxurious comfort, allowing you to reconnect with nature without sacrificing modern amenities.

With panoramic vistas of the Madhya Pradesh highlands and access to pristine trekking trails, Sol Retreat provides the perfect balance of adventure and relaxation. Our expert naturalists offer guided experiences that reveal the hidden wonders of this biodiverse region.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Welcome

+

Begin your journey with our signature welcome ritual, featuring local herbal tea and traditional treats. After settling into your eco-luxury suite, join us for a gentle orientation walk around the property to familiarize yourself with your surroundings.

In the evening, enjoy a specially curated welcome dinner prepared with ingredients from our organic garden, followed by a briefing about the activities planned during your stay. As night falls, gather around the community fire pit for stargazing and stories about the region's natural and cultural heritage.

Day 2: Forest Immersion

+

Wake up to birdsong and join our optional sunrise yoga session on the panoramic deck. After a nourishing breakfast, set off on a guided forest walk with our resident naturalist who will introduce you to the rich biodiversity of the region.

Return for lunch and a period of rest or personal exploration. In the afternoon, choose between a hands-on workshop learning traditional crafts from local artisans or a more challenging trek to a nearby viewpoint. The day concludes with a farm-to-table dinner and an evening of acoustic music around the campfire.

Day 3: Wellness & Culture

+

Your third day focuses on holistic well-being and cultural immersion. Begin with a guided meditation session, followed by a specially prepared Ayurvedic breakfast. Spend the morning enjoying a personalized wellness treatment using locally sourced herbs and essential oils.

After lunch, visit a nearby village community where you'll participate in traditional daily activities, from cooking to crafts, learning directly from the families who have called these mountains home for generations. Return to Sol Retreat for a special dinner featuring regional delicacies, followed by cultural performances by local musicians.

Important Information

  • Sol Retreat is located at an altitude of 7,000 feet; we recommend a day of acclimatization for guests coming from lower altitudes.
  • The retreat practices sustainable tourism with solar power, rainwater harvesting, and waste management systems. We encourage guests to be mindful of water and energy usage.
  • Weather can be variable in the mountains; we recommend packing layers even during summer months.
  • Cell phone reception may be limited in certain areas of the property, allowing for a true digital detox experience.
  • Our kitchen can accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice.
  • The nearest medical facility is 30 minutes away; we maintain basic first aid equipment and trained staff on-site.

Kheoni Retreat - 3 Days / 2 Nights

Escape to the heart of Madhya Pradesh with a luxurious two-day retreat at Kheoni Retreat and Kheoni Wildlife Sanctuary—where nature, culture, and comfort come together seamlessly.

From thrilling tiger safaris and ancient temple visits to private plunge pools shaded by mango trees and evenings under the stars, this curated getaway offers the perfect balance of adventure and relaxation.

Whether you're seeking serene moments or unforgettable wildlife encounters, this retreat promises a deeply immersive forest experience.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Welcome

+

• Land in Indore (morning/afternoon flight).

• Luxurious spa treatment for 2 (60 mins) followed by lavish multi-cuisine buffet spread at Indore's premium 5-star hotel.

• 1.5 hours of travel to the recently excavated Devbadla temples from Indore via road followed by arrival at the Tiger Safari Lodge.

• Arrive at the Devbadla temples dating back to 1000 AD.

• Accompanied by a guide to show around and explain the historical importance of the temples and the area.

• 30 min ride to the Tiger Safari Lodge.

• Arrive at the Tiger Safari Lodge.

• Rooms with attached private plunge pool.

• Open-air dinner and drinks under a mango tree.

Day 2: Forest Immersion

+

• Leave the Tiger Safari Lodge by 5:30AM for an early morning totally private safari to a pristine, untouched area of the Kheoni Sanctuary.

• Have light breakfast on the way to the sanctuary.(Optional)

• Enjoy the adrenaline filled safari and head back to the retreat.

• Brunch at the lodge including local delicacies.

• Leave for the safari at 2:15pm to reach the Southern Circuit gate by 3:30 pm.

• Arrive at the gate at 3:30pm and witness the magnificent tigress, Meera, in the meadows of Kheoni Wildlife Sanctuary.

• Ride back to the hotel and arrive by 7:15PM.

• Folk music performance under the stars and mango tree shade.

• Local delicacies and snacks for dinner.

• Open bar and drinks served throughout the evening.

• Have dinner and relax in your private plunge pool.

Day 3: Take Back Memories

+

• Wake up to the sounds of nature and enjoy a filling breakfast.

• Departure gift of sustainable lavender eye pillows for in-flight relaxation.

• Drop to Indore airport/Bhopal.

Important Information

  • The retreat practices sustainable tourism with solar power, rainwater harvesting, and waste management systems. We encourage guests to be mindful of water and energy usage.
  • Weather can be variable in the mountains; we recommend packing layers even during summer months.
  • Cell phone reception may be limited in certain areas of the property, allowing for a true digital detox experience.
  • Our kitchen can accommodate dietary restrictions with advance notice.
  • The nearest medical facility is 30 minutes away; we maintain basic first aid equipment and trained staff on-site.

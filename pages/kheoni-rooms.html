Sustainability Features

Our cabins are designed with environmental consciousness as a primary focus. Each accommodation incorporates sustainable technologies and practices that minimize our impact on Kheoni's delicate ecosystem:

  • ♻️ Solar-powered electricity and water heating
  • 💧 Rainwater harvesting and greywater recycling systems
  • 🌱 Construction using reclaimed and sustainable materials
  • 🍃 Natural ventilation design reducing energy needs
  • 🗑️ Zero-waste initiatives with composting and recycling
  • 🌿 Native landscaping that supports local wildlife
